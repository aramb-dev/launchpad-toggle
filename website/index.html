FAQ

Does this modify system files? ▾

It writes or removes a feature‑flag plist at /Library/Preferences/FeatureFlags/Domain/SpotlightUI.plist, with a backup.

Will I always need to restart? ▾

A full restart is the most reliable way for the change to take effect across the system.

Is it reversible? ▾

Yes. You can switch back anytime; your previous setting is backed up with a timestamp.

What about the new Spotlight features? ▾

Important caveat: Enabling Classic Launchpad will also revert the revamped macOS Spotlight to its older version. For a supercharged Spotlight experience, we recommend Raycast — a powerful launcher that enhances your productivity beyond what system Spotlight offers.
